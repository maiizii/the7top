The Unknown Kidnapper

Seven clues. One story. One truth.

Opening

Welcome to The Seventh Game. A tycoon’s son is kidnapped. The ransom demand is strange.

  1. Clue One — The Ransom Demand

    The ransom—one million dollars, paid entirely in old, used bills.

  2. Clue Two — Postal Instructions

    The money must be sent via the postal service inside a plain, ordinary wrapper.

  3. Clue Three — A Curious Wrapper

    The wrapper must come from the tycoon’s own office supply.

  4. Clue Four — Address Check

    Investigators confirm only one part of the address is real—the city.

  5. Clue Five — False Details

    The rest of the address is fake. The street name and recipient do not exist.

  6. Clue Six — Impossible Delivery

    How can the kidnapper collect a package mailed to a completely fake address?

  7. Clue Seven — Postal Rule

    By rule, any undeliverable package is simply returned to the sender.
